About Ever-GreenPath

At Ever-GreenPath, we are dedicated to empowering communities to build resilience against climate change. Our platform provides innovative tools and resources to help individuals, organizations, and policymakers understand climate risks and develop sustainable strategies for the future. By harnessing the power of data-driven insights and community engagement, we aim to create a more resilient and sustainable world for generations to come.

Air Pollution and AQI

In addition to climate resilience, Ever-GreenPath is committed to addressing air pollution challenges. We provide information and tools related to Air Quality Index (AQI) to raise awareness about air quality and its impact on health and the environment. Through our platform, users can access real-time AQI data, learn about air pollution sources, and discover ways to reduce their exposure to harmful pollutants.
